## S2 brake lights

Modes:

* LOW: lower brightness (constantly on by default), works on 6-12V provided to pin 2
* HIGH: high brightness (breaking indicator), works on 12V provided to pin 3, overrides LOW

Specs:

* 6 - 12V
* ~0.16W @ 6V-LOW , ~1.72W @ 12V-HIGH
* LED
